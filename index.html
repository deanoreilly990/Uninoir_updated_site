Welcome to UniNoir

I wanted to build a space where people felt safe and comfortable to learn and connect with others. There was a time where i battled with depression and i told myself if i could get through this i would create an environment to teach people the importance of mental health and give them the guidance and support they deserved. Internal growth and self actualization is a topic people take for granted and it is ultimately what determines your success, I have been blessed with mentors and tutors who have educated me to the level that i can educate others. All events and workshops have must have a good intention such as raising money for charity, supporting communities or creating awareness.

Uni Noirs motto is to inform, inspire and impart.

Darkness is not an entity it is simply the absent of light, let us help you find your light.

What UniNoir offers

Unioir has a Mission, to helps you Grow and Connect with Motivational Workshops, Coaching and Events. Our mission is to educate & Inspire you to live your most authentic life.
